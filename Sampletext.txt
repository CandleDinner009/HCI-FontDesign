Welcome to the Andromeda Galaxy – 2.5 million light years from Earth. You’re the Pathfinder: leader of a squad of explorers tasked to find a home for humanity in this strange system. Andromeda holds dangers and challenges greater than anything you’ve ever faced. With the hope of our entire existence on your shoulders, you alone must blaze the path for humanity.
    Experience a vast galaxy, full of open worlds waiting to be discovered. Delve into the rich character customization with a classless progression system that allows you the freedom to chart your own course for the Pathfinder. Craft a party of skilled allies and help humanity write its next chapter in Andromeda.
